# Echoes of Fear
The final playable build of my 2024 horror game project.

[WATCH THE PLAYTHROUGH HERE!!] 
(https://github.com/link395/Echoes-of-Fear/releases/download/v1.0/Echoes_of_Fear_Playthrough.mp4)


Echo wakes up in his worst nightmare: his own home, now crawling with monsters.

Assisted only by the wise words of his trusty spider companion, Hambone, and mysterious "Sweet T", Echo must gain enough strength to navigate and conquer a transformed reality.

Will you help Echo hide, fight, and escape, or will he be consumed by the shadows?
